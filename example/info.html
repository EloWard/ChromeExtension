User Guide

Linking Ranks

Unranked accounts cannot be linked.

League of Legends and TeamFight Tactics

  • To link your League of Legends and/or TeamFight Tactics ranks, follow these steps:
    1. Navigate to the Riot Games Account website
    2. Sign in to your Riot account
    3. Your ranks will be automatically linked to your account
    4. Navigate to twitch.tv and start chatting in a League of Legends or TeamFight Tactics stream to see your ranks!

Dota 2

  • To link your Dota 2 rank, follow these steps:
    1. Navigate to the Steam Community website
    2. Sign in to your Steam account
    3. Make sure your Steam account is public and your Dota match data is exposed.
    4. Your rank will be automatically linked to your account. If you have a leaderboard rank it will also show under your rank in chat.
    5. Navigate to twitch.tv and start chatting in a Dota 2 stream to see your rank!

Chess

  • To link your Chess rank, follow these steps:
    1. Sign in to chess.com
    2. Your rank will be automatically linked to your account
    3. Navigate to twitch.tv and start chatting in a Chess stream to see your ranks!

Using the Pop-up

The pop-up displays all your linked ranks for the currently implemented games. After linking a rank to your account, use the pop-up to easily verify if it was linked successfully. You can use the refresh button located next to each rank (If your rank has been reset to unranked, your previous rank will be displayed until you are ranked again). If you would like to remove or unlink a rank from your account, you can simply click the 'Trash' button located next to the refresh button on each rank.

Premium

With the monthly premium subscription, you can display your rank even when watching streams that aren't available to link (e.g., 'Just Chatting' streams or other non-linked games). Premium users can also choose to set their League of Legends/TFT rank emblems to legacy or relic emblems if they prefer. (Note: There are no Relic emblems for Iron, Emerald, or Grandmaster. There are no Legacy emblems for Emerald). To subscribe, click here to get a license key and then click the 'Go Premium' button to enter the license key!

How Ranks are Displayed

Once your ranks are successfully linked and visible within the pop-up, the corresponding ranked emblem will appear next to your name in Twitch chat, but only while you are on a stream with the stream category of that specific game. For instance, if you've linked your TeamFight Tactics rank, it will be displayed exclusively on TeamFight Tactics streams and the same goes for League of Legends or Chess for their respective ranks. Therefore, even with a premium subscription and TeamFight Tactics set as your default rank, your TeamFight Tactics rank will not be visible on these specific League of Legends or Chess streams. It will, however, be displayed on streams with any and every other stream category such as 'Just Chatting' or 'ASMR'.
