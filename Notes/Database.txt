- Database Name: eloward
- Database ID: ef228f37-9c28-43f2-a211-cfddd4188c78

## Tables

### subscribed-channels
- Purpose: Stores channel identifiers
- Columns:
  - `id` (INTEGER): Primary key, auto-incrementing
  - `channel_id` (TEXT): External channel identifier, unique, indexed
  - `subscription_date` (TIMESTAMP): When the channel was subscribed to
